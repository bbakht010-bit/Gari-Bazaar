Privacy Policy

Last updated May 2026. This summary is provided for transparency; replace with lawyer-reviewed text before scaling production.

GariBazaar (“we”, “us”) operates the website and dealer tools. By using our service you agree this policy describes how information may be handled as we integrate authentication, hosting, and analytics.

Information we collect

When dealers register we may collect business name, contact details, showroom address, listing content, and (after Phase 2) account credentials backed by Firebase. Buyers browsing the public site may generate standard logs (IP, browser type) via hosting/CDN providers.

How we use data

  • To display dealership profiles and listings to buyers.
  • To authenticate dealers and operate dashboards.
  • To improve search, spam prevention, and (later) analytics you opt into.

Storage & security

Planned production stack uses Firebase Authentication, Firestore, and Storage with rules restricting writes to authenticated dealer accounts. No system is perfectly secure — use strong passwords and protect API keys.

Third parties

Maps, fonts, image CDNs, email delivery, and payment processors (when enabled) have their own policies. We only integrate services needed to run the marketplace.

Your choices

Dealers may request profile or listing corrections by contacting support. Regulatory rights under Pakistani law apply as interpreted by counsel.

Contact

Privacy questions: Contact page or mailto configured on that page.
