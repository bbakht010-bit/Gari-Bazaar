About GariBazaar

We are building Pakistan’s verified dealer-first marketplace — so buyers find real inventory from trusted showrooms, not anonymous listings.

Our mission

GariBazaar connects serious car buyers with professional dealerships across Pakistan. Every listing is tied to a registered dealer profile, so you know who you are dealing with before you pick up the phone or visit the lot.

Where we operate

We launched with Islamabad and Rawalpindi dealers and expand city by city. Our roadmap includes Lahore, Faisalabad, and beyond as more verified dealerships join.

Why dealer-only?

Buyer trust and post-sale accountability matter — especially for used cars. By focusing on licensed dealers and employees, we can build tools for showroom teams: dashboards, subscriptions, ratings, and (coming soon) in-app inquiries.

For dealers

  • Public profile pages and searchable inventory.
  • Growth tiers for visibility and listings (pricing on the homepage).
  • Coming next: Firebase-backed accounts, real-time inventory sync, and image hosting.
